About Connectify

Connectify is a platform designed for students at CBIT to connect with each other. Whether you're looking for research collaborators, project partners, or simply friends for entertainment, Connectify makes it easy to find like-minded individuals and collaborate on exciting opportunities.

We aim to create a strong community of students across all branches to foster innovation and personal growth.
